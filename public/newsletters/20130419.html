News from the site

What an amazing week! We've rolled out one of the main new features: single sign-on against Github. Yes,it doens't sound like a lot, but now, if you use it the following will happen:

  1. Your public repos will be available for you on your profile page and you'll be able to publish them on the site with a single clickl
  2. For every repository published on LFPR, we'll be gathering commit data, which means we'll be able to track your work, so expect news regarding this in the near future!

Also, a big shout-out to @elecay, who's been helping us with the site fixing bugs and contributing excellent ideas: Thanks man, we really appreciate it!
